Margarita Grinvald

I am a Computer Vision engineer at Meta (previously Facebook).

Recently, I completed my PhD in robotic perception at ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Roland Siegwart.

I have received my Master's degree in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich in 2017. Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Informatics from USI Lugano in 2014.
